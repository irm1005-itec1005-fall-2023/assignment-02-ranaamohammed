ABOUT THE CAFE

Cat Cafe was founded by a cat and coffee lover who want wanted to spend time with cats as a living. The cafe is a place where you can enjoy a cup of coffee and play with cats to destress from an exhausting day at work. All of our cats are rescued from the streets and are well taken care of. We hope you enjoy your time.


opening hours: 9am - 4pm.
address: 1234 Cat Street, Vancouver, BC, Canada
